Victoria Miluch

Victoria Miluch is a fiction writer and translator. She is the recipient of a Fulbright fellowship to Poland and has served as a Peace Corps volunteer in Mexico. Born in San Francisco and raised in Arizona, she currently lives outside of Washington, DC, with her family. DEADLANDS (Lake Union 2023) is her first novel.

Victoria is represented by Kerry D'Agostino at Curtis Brown.
